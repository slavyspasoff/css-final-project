Restaurant Little Pharaoh

Taste the ancient

Our Intention

We would like to bring you closer to the flair of Egyptian culture and that is why we cordially invite you to test our restaurant and let our traditional dishes spoil your palate. We wish you some pleasant hours in our restaurant.

Egyptian Cuisine

We present you a colorful selection from the fine cuisine of the egyptian culture. With the use of vegetables, legumes and fruits with rice, hummus, falafel, shawarma, kebab and kofta. Also beans, kushari, eish baladi and much more... Specialties from the grill, from the pan and from the oven, original oriental wine selection and the traditional arrack.

Our Team

Our team consists of 27 employees, all of whom are qualified. Our chef cook has lived in Egypt for a number of years and enjoys creating a work of art out of food for guests every day. Our waiters will always do their best to fulfill every of your wishes.
